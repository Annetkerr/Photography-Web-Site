About Me

I was born and raised in Connecticut and moved to the Bay Area in 2018. I started taking photography classes at City College of San Francisco in 2020. Photography has been a lifelong passion — one I am finally pursuing full time after a long career in enterprise technology.

I think of my work as a way of exploring the world through the camera. I capture what I see and take it home with me, drawn to details that don't reveal themselves immediately. When I do a close read of a scene, I often notice something that eluded me in the moment. I spend as much time working the image as I do making it. That is when the discovery begins, and pixels become art.
